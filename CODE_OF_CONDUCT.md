# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

我们承诺为所有参与者提供无骚扰的社区环境，不论年龄、体型、残障状况、
民族、性别认同、经验水平、教育背景、国籍、宗教信仰或性取向。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

**Positive behaviors / 积极行为：**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language / 使用友善包容的语言
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints / 尊重不同观点
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism / 接受建设性批评
- Focusing on what is best for the community / 以社区利益为重

**Unacceptable behaviors / 不可接受的行为：**

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments / 侮辱性言论
- Public or private harassment / 骚扰行为
- Publishing others' private information / 未经授权公开他人隐私
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate / 其他不当行为
